Who We Are

Established in Dubai in 2015, Extravaganza epitomizes a rich heritage in luxury perfumery. With a deep understanding of the beauty industry and a commitment to excellence, Extravaganza has grown into a leading name in manufacturing and distributing premium fragrances. Guided by a passion for innovation and quality, the brand continues to set benchmarks in the world of luxury fragrance, offering exceptional creations that captivate and inspire.

Leadership Excellence

Extravaganza has emerged as a dominant player in the Middle East's luxury and prestige beauty scene. The company proudly trades and markets over 50 international brands spreading its reach far and wide from the Middle East and Europe to the Americas and Africa.
